Our Members

Juan David Granada Mejía

PhD in Information Systems Engineering from the Universidad Rey Juan Carlos, Computer Engineering from the Università degli Studi di Padova (Italy), Master in Information Technologies and Computer Systems, Master in Decision Engineering. Pre-doctoral researcher at the Politecnico di Milano - Polo Regionale di Como (Italy). Currently, he is Associate Professor and Researcher at the Universidad Rey Juan Carlos

He has worked as a computer scientist and technical developer for Hewlett-Packard in different cities in Northern Italy. He has taught courses specialising in Business Intelligence for different companies such as BBVA, ANIDA, GALP, HAYA, Rent & Tech, Clínica CEMTRO, COFARES, SPB Seguros, Grupo Planeta, among others, and has been Director of the Technology Area of IDE-CESEM, Instituto de Directivos de Empresa, Business School. He has participated in several research projects and has several publications in scientific forums both nationally and internationally, as well as in prestigious journals in the field of Software Engineering.
