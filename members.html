Our Members

Juan David Granada Mejía

PhD in Information Systems Engineering from the Universidad Rey Juan Carlos, Computer Engineering from the Università degli Studi di Padova (Italy), Master in Information Technologies and Computer Systems, Master in Decision Engineering. Pre-doctoral researcher at the Politecnico di Milano - Polo Regionale di Como (Italy). Currently, he is Associate Professor and Researcher at the Universidad Rey Juan Carlos

He has worked as a computer scientist and technical developer for Hewlett-Packard in different cities in Northern Italy. He has taught courses specialising in Business Intelligence for different companies such as BBVA, ANIDA, GALP, HAYA, Rent & Tech, Clínica CEMTRO, COFARES, SPB Seguros, Grupo Planeta, among others, and has been Director of the Technology Area of IDE-CESEM, Instituto de Directivos de Empresa, Business School. He has participated in several research projects and has several publications in scientific forums both nationally and internationally, as well as in prestigious journals in the field of Software Engineering.

Juan Manuel Vara Mesa

Computer Engineer, Master in Information Technology and Computer Systems and PhD in Computer Engineering. ITIL, CMMi and Scrum Master certifications. Pre-doctoral researcher at INRIA at the University of Nantes and post-doctoral researcher at ERISS (Europen Research Institute on Service Science) at the University of Tilburg.

Director of the Master's Degree in Information Systems Engineering and Coordinator of the research line in Software Engineering, Information Systems and Services of the PhD programme in ICT at the same University.

His main lines of research have been Model Driven Engineering, Software Project Management, Information Systems and Service Engineering, in which context he has contributed to the creation and launch of the first Degree in Service Engineering, which he coordinated for more than 4 years.

Francisco Javier Pérez Blanco

PhD in Information and Communication Technologies from the Universidad Rey Juan Carlos. Graduated in Computer Engineering and Master in Information Systems Engineering at the same University. He currently holds the position of Assistant Professor Doctor, work that he combines with his research work with several publications in various forums both nationally and internationally.

In the past he has worked as an IT consultant for companies such as Deloitte.

Cristian Gómez Macías
