About Us

Welcome to CommIT, a research group dedicated to explore and develop innovative solutions in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology. Our team is composed of experts in diverse areas, including Model Driven Engineering, Data Science, Business Models, Blockchain and Smart Contracts, Process Mining, and Agile Methodologies.

Our mission is to advance knowledge and practice in these fields, collaborating with academic institutions and industry to apply our research in the real world. Through research projects, collaborations and teaching, we seek to positively impact society..
